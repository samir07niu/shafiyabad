⚖️ Aam Aadmi Ke 30 Kanooni Adhikar (Know Your Rights)

Kanoon sabke liye barabar hai. Apne adhikar padhein taaki koi aapko pareshan na kar sake:

🛡️ Police aur FIR Se Jude Adhikar

1. Zero FIR: Crime kahin bhi hua ho, aap desh ke kisi bhi Police Station me FIR kara sakte hain. Police ye nahi keh sakti ki 'Ye humara ilaqe nahi hai'.
2. FIR Darj Na Hone Par: Agar thanedar FIR na likhe, toh aap SP (Superintendent of Police) ko post se shikayat bhej sakte hain (CrPC Sec 154).
3. Giraftari Ka Karan: Arrest karte waqt Police ko aapko karan (reason) batana padega (CrPC Sec 50). Bina karan arrest gair-kanooni hai.
4. 24 Ghante Ka Niyam: Arrest hone ke 24 ghante ke andar Police ko aapko Magistrate ke samne pesh karna hi padega.
5. Muft Wakeel (Free Legal Aid): Agar aap garib hain, toh kanoon aapko free sarkari wakeel dene ka adhikar deta hai (Article 39A).
6. Hathkadi (Handcuffs) Niyam: Normal mamlo me Police aapko Hathkadi nahi laga sakti jab tak judge ka order na ho.
7. Search Warrant: Bina Search Warrant ke Police aapke ghar ki talashi nahi le sakti (kuch sangeen mamlo ko chhod kar).

👩‍🦰 Mahilaon Ke Adhikar (Women's Rights)

8. Giraftari Ka Samay: Kisi bhi mahila ko sham 6 baje ke baad aur subah 6 baje se pehle giraftar nahi kiya ja sakta (CrPC Sec 46).
9. Mahila Police Zaroori: Mahila ko sirf ek Mahila Police aadhikari hi arrest kar sakti hai. Purush police haath nahi laga sakta.
10. Online FIR: Mahilayein email ya post ke zariye bhi FIR darj kara sakti hain, unhe thane jane ki zaroorat nahi hai.
11. Saman Vetan (Equal Pay): Ek hi kaam ke liye purush aur mahila ko barabar salary milni chahiye (Equal Remuneration Act).
12. Paitrik Sampatti (Property): Pita ki sampatti me beti ka bhi utna hi haq hai jitna bete ka (Hindu Succession Act).

🚦 Traffic Aur Sadak Suraksha

13. Gadi Ki Chabi (Keys): Traffic police ya koi hawaldar aapki gadi ki chabi (keys) nikal nahi sakta. Ye kanoonan jurm hai.
14. DigiLocker Valid Hai: Agar aapke paas DigiLocker ya mParivahan app me Driving License / RC hai, toh police original nahi mang sakti (IT Act).
15. Double Challan: Ek hi galti (jaise bina helmet) ke liye din mein 2 baar challan nahi kat sakta (Note: overspeeding alag hai).
16. Good Samaritan Law: Agar aap kisi ghayal ko aspatal le jate hain, toh na police pareshan karegi, na aspatal wale paise mangenge.
17. Drink & Drive: Agar blood alcohol 30mg/100ml se zyada hai, toh bina warrant giraftari ho sakti hai.

💻 Cyber Crime Aur IT Act (Digital Rights)

18. Fake ID Banana (Impersonation): Kisi ke naam ki fake Facebook/Insta id banana jurm hai. 3 saal ki jail ho sakti hai (IT Act Sec 66D).
19. Photo Viral Karna (Sec 66E): Bina permission kisi ki private photo khichna ya internet par dalna sangeen jurm hai.
20. Cyber Stalking (Pichha Karna): Internet par kisi ladki ko baar-baar message karke pareshan karna Cyber Stalking hai (IPC 354D).
21. Hacking & Wi-Fi Chori: Kisi aur ka network ya account bina puche hack karna ya use karna jurm hai (IT Act Sec 43/66).
22. OTP Fraud Liability: Agar aapke bank se bina aapki galti paisa katta hai, aur aap 3 din me bank ko batate hain, toh pura paisa bank wapas dega (RBI Rule).
23. Child Pornography: Bacchon se jude ashleel video dekhna, rakhna ya share karna gair-jamanati jurm hai (Sec 67B). Sidhe jail hogi.
24. Online Dhamki: Social media par gaali-galoch ya maarne ki dhamki dena Cyber Defamation (Sec 499) aur IPC 506 ke tahat jurm hai.

🏦 Aam Aadmi Ke Adhikar (Banking & Consumer)

25. MRP Rule: Koi bhi dukaandar aapse kisi saaman par likhe hue M.R.P. (Maximum Retail Price) se ek rupya zyada nahi le sakta.
26. Hotel Me Pani: Aap kisi bhi 5-star ya normal hotel me muft pani peene aur washroom (toilet) use karne ja sakte hain (Indian Sarai Act).
